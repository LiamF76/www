About Us

Welcome to Daraan Games, the premier source for all your D&D supplementary needs! Founded by an unlikely team of three raccoons in a trench coat pretending to be a college student at Concord University, we bring you resources for your game that you can't get anywhere else!

The brains (and paws) behind the operation, Rufus, Patches, and Sprocket, started this venture in the back of a Sheetz after realizing their shared love of storytelling and shiny objects. With an eye for detail and a knack for chaos, the trio has been crafting unique monsters, homebrew maps, and magical items ever since.
